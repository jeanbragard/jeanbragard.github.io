Research interests

Computational modeling in broad sense with special emphasis on Biomedical applications.
Specific topics include:

  • Connexins dynamics in cardiac tissue;
  • Numerical simulations of defibrillation;
  • Modelization of electric waves in the heart;
  • Analysis of Biomedical data;
  • Modelization of crystal growth in undercooled melts;
  • Control and synchronization of spatially extended chaotic systems;
  • Dynamics of open Bose-Einstein condensates;
  • Pattern formation in nonlinear optics;
  • Instabilities in fluid mechanics (patterns and waves).

Ph.D. and Master thesis positions are available in our group.
